# Unified Course Platform - Standard Demo Script

**Target Audience:** General developers, hackathon judges, or peers.
**Duration:** ~3 minutes

## 1. Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Action:** Open the landing page.
- **Script:** "Welcome to CourseAI. Traditional e-learning is static and one-size-fits-all. We built a platform that dynamically generates interactive courses tailored to your exact knowledge gaps using a Multi-Agent AI architecture."

## 2. Core Generation (0:30 - 1:30)
- **Action:** Log in via Auth0. Click "Create Course". Enter "Distributed Systems in Go".
- **Script:** "Instead of pre-recorded videos, the platform streams the curriculum in real-time. Notice the Server-Sent Events (SSE) streaming the markdown instantly—cutting latency from 45 seconds to just 2 seconds. The AI also embeds relevant YouTube videos on the fly."

## 3. Interactive Learning (1:30 - 2:15)
- **Action:** Open the AI Tutor sidebar and ask a question.
- **Script:** "If I get stuck, I don't need to leave the app. The AI Tutor has full context of the lesson I'm reading. Furthermore, the platform automatically generates Flashcards and Practice Labs based on the content."

## 4. Analytics & Community (2:15 - 3:00)
- **Action:** Navigate to the Analytics Dashboard, then Community Templates.
- **Script:** "We track every minute studied and every quiz taken, presenting it in a GitHub-style heatmap. Finally, courses can be published to the Community Hub, where the best content rises to the top of the Leaderboard."
